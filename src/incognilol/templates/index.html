IncogniLOL is a free and open source front end for 9GAG that is lightweight, uses modern formats (such as webp instead of jpg and webm instead of mp4) to save on bandwidth and does not include any tracking elements of 9GAG.

Currently, it is in very early development and does not support browsing the homepage or tags. However to use this service on individual posts, you can:

9gag.com/gag/xyzabce -> ilol.m3r.one/gag/xyzabce

If you encounter any errors, please open an issue.
